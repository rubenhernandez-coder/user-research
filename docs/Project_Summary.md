# Study AI — Project Summary
*Ruben Hernandez · May 2026*

---

## What Is This Project?

**Study AI** is a web-based scheduling platform designed specifically for students ages 13–25. The core idea: instead of just listing deadlines like a calendar or to-do list, Study AI automatically builds a personalized, day-by-day study plan that breaks work into manageable sessions spread across the time available before each deadline.

---

## The Problem

Most students struggle with time management — not because they're lazy, but because the skill is genuinely hard to develop, especially for teenagers whose brains (specifically the prefrontal cortex, responsible for planning and self-regulation) aren't fully developed until ages 23–25.

Key stats backing the problem:
- 87% of high school and college students identify as self-proclaimed procrastinators
- 45% say procrastination regularly hurts their grades
- 31% sleep 5 hours or less per night due to last-minute work
- Students spend 3–4 hours daily on social media, competing with study time
- Digital scheduling tools have been shown to improve on-time completion by 35% and reduce academic stress by 28%

---

## The Solution

Students enter their assignments, quizzes, and exams — along with due dates and daily availability. Study AI then:

- **Generates a balanced study schedule** spread across available days
- **Avoids overloading any single day** by distributing work intelligently
- **Sends reminders** as deadlines approach
- **Adapts automatically** when schedules change or new tasks are added
- **Tracks progress** so students can see what's done and what's left

The key differentiator: **most apps show what's due — Study AI tells you what to do today.**

---

## Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| Smart Schedule Builder | Auto-generates a balanced study plan across all active deadlines |
| Deadline Tracker | Visual dashboard sorted by urgency |
| Reminders & Alerts | Timely notifications to keep students on track |
| Workload Balancer | Flags overloaded days and redistributes tasks |
| Adaptive Rescheduling | Adjusts the plan when sessions are missed or new tasks are added |
| Progress Tracking | Visual completion tracking to build motivation |

---

## Target Users

High school students, college students, and online/hybrid learners — anyone in the 13–25 age range who is managing multiple deadlines without enough external structure.

---

## What Makes It Different

Existing tools (myHomework, MyStudyLife, Google Calendar, Todoist) are **passive** — they hold information but don't act on it. Study AI is **active**: it takes a student's full workload and turns it into a clear action plan.
